Rate of Deaths VS Gun Law Safety

The x-axis is each state's gun safety rank, and the y-axis is each state's gun death rate.

The safety rank is a ranking system that displays each state's level of gun safety according to their laws. 1 is very safe/strict, while 50 is less safe/strict.
